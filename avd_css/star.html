what is 'star'

"You are now witness to something great, do you get" star is 2hollis's fourth studio album. Following the singles, "afraid" and "style". Star released on April 4th 2025. Star was recorded in the burned remains of Hollis's childhood home that was burnt in the LA fires of January 2025. The reason for the name according to Hollis was, "the only thing to survive (the fire) was “The star”. my mother had it framed.. i knew it was destiny." He added, "burn was one of the last songs finished.. the next day we had to evacuate."
